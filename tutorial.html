Landfill tutorial

Hi! This is a research application that helps gather more information about landfills across the United States. Currently, there are 1235 landfill across the country. Through this application, we want to identify the total square footage of all landfills in the country as well as gather more information about the areas surrounding landfills.

This is a research project being conducted through Open Trash Lab, an organization that strives to improve our waste practices across the country by better understanding the current waste system. Identifying landfills and understanding them better is a step towards connecting people with their waste and understanding the life of their trash.

To begin, you will be presented the latitude and longitude of a landfill collected from the Environmental Protection Agency.

This data may contain errors!!!

Often the latitude and longitude of a landfill may relate to an office or nearby building and not the landfill itself.

As this is a demo application, you can also draw polygons. Polygons could be used to measure areas in the maps.
